About Us

Sangli Miraj Commercial Ventures Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2022 by individuals with over 50 years collective experience in venture capital and private equity.SMCVPL endeavours to support its mission through value added investments and through providing business and technological guidance, mentoring and leveraging it's vast network of relationships with Industry, academia, research institutions, entrepreneurs and investors. SMCVPL focuses primarily on food / agro processing, engineering, pharmaceutical & real estate sectors as a niche to mobilize resources in these domain. It closely connects with companies and hand hold it to the growth trajectory.

Management Team has :

  • Completed over 10 PIPEs (private placements in listed companies on the BSE / NSE)
  • 2 Reverse Mergers
  • 1 IPO
  • Over 20 investments and exits in start-ups over 20 years
  • Deployed over 200Cr into public markets and over 30Cr in private companies
