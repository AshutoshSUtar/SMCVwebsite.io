About Us

Sangli Miraj Commercial Ventures Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2022 by individuals with over 50 years of collective experience in venture capital and private equity, SMCVPL endeavors to support its mission through value-added investments and by providing business and technological guidance, mentoring, and leveraging its vast network of relationships with industry, academia, research institutions, entrepreneurs, and investors.

SMCVPL focuses primarily on food/agro processing, engineering, pharmaceutical, and real estate sectors as a niche to mobilize resources in these domains. It closely connects with companies and helps guide them on the growth trajectory.

  • Invest in undervalued small and microcaps.
  • Special situations investing, such as demergers, reverse mergers, and risk arbitrage.
  • Invest in small companies and help them grow.
  • PIPE Investments.
